Classic-Steak

The staple for the hungry individual

Ingredients

  • Sandwhich Steaks
  • Potatoes
  • Asparagus
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Lay steaks flat on chopping board
  2. Season both sides with salt & pepper
  3. Wash and slice Potatoes into quarters
  4. Wash Asparagus
  5. Place Asparagus and Potatoes on oven tray
  6. Set oven to 180 degrees and leave for 20 minutes
  7. Heat up pan on stove with max heat
  8. Add butter when warm
  9. Move butter around pan to coat base
  10. Lay steaks into pan evenly spaced apart
  11. Flip steaks after approximately 3-4 minutes
  12. Remove Tray from oven and allow to cool
  13. Remove pan from heat and serve steaks
  14. Garnish plates with Asparagus and Potatoes
  15. Add salt and pepper to taste
