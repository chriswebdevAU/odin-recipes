Penne-Passata

Cheap to make, and serves several without skimping on taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Brown Onion
  • 1 Red Capsicum
  • 1 Green Capsicum
  • 1 Bottle of Passata
  • 1 Bunch of Basil
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Chicken stock
  • 500g Lean Mince
  • 500g Penne Pasta
  • OLive Oil

Instructions

  1. Dice Onions
  2. Dice Capsicums
  3. Dice Basil
  4. Heat pan on high
  5. Drizzle pan with olive oil
  6. add diced ingredients
  7. add mince
  8. stir until mince turns brown
  9. add 200ml chicken stock
  10. when stock begins to evaporate/boil add passata
  11. stir thoroughly on a high heat for 5-10 minutes
  12. and another 100ml of chicken stock>
  13. turn heat down to low and let simmer
  14. Boil kettle and add 2 litres of boiled water to a pot
  15. add penne to pot
  16. salt the pot so the penne doesn't stick to it and burn
  17. take penne off heat when its soft when poked with a fork
  18. drain pot of water and add penne to simmering sauce
  19. Serve
