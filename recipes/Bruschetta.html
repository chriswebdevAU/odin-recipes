Bruschetta

Bruschetta, the perfect snack, or entree before a meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive Olive
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • French loaf or round loaf
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Cut Loaf into quarter inch slices and place on oven tray
  2. Spread Crushed Garlic onto slices evenly
  3. drizzle with olive oil
  4. Place in oven at 180 degrees until golden
  5. Remove from oven
  6. dice tomatoes and onion
  7. using a spoon, distribute over slices
  8. drizzle with balsamic and serve!
