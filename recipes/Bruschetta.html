Bruschetta

Bruschetta, the perfect snack or entrée before a meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • French loaf or round loaf
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Cut loaf into quarter-inch slices and place on oven tray
  2. Spread crushed garlic onto slices evenly
  3. Drizzle with olive oil
  4. Place in oven at 180°C until golden
  5. Remove from oven
  6. Dice tomatoes and onion
  7. Using a spoon, distribute mixture over slices
  8. Drizzle with balsamic and serve!
